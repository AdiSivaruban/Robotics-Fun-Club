How to Build a Paddle Boat

Materials Needed

  • 1 empty plastic water bottle (with cap)
  • 3 popsicle sticks
  • 1 strong rubber band
  • Tape

Instructions

Step 1: Use the plastic bottle as the boat body. Make sure it floats and is sealed tightly. Decorate it to make it uniquely yours!
Step 2: Tape or glue two popsicle sticks parallel to each other on the sides near the back of the bottle. Leave a gap between them for your paddle to spin.
Step 3: Stretch the rubber band between the two popsicle sticks so it hangs across the gap. Make sure it‚Äôs snug but not too tight.
Step 4: Cut one popsicle stick in half. Insert it through the middle of the rubber band and tape it in place.
Step 5: Wind the paddle backward to tighten the rubber band. Release and watch your boat go!
