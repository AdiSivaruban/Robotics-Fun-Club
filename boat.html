Project: Paddle Boat

Make a simple self-paddling boat using a water bottle and rubber band!

Materials Needed:

  • 1 empty plastic water bottle (with cap)
  • 3 popsicle sticks
  • 1 strong rubber band
  • Tape

Instructions:

Step 1: Use the plastic bottle as the boat body. Make sure it floats and is tightly sealed. Decorate if you want!

Step 2: Tape two popsicle sticks parallel on either side of the back of the bottle, leaving space between them.

Step 3: Stretch the rubber band between the two sticks.

Step 4: Cut a popsicle stick in half and slide one half into the center of the rubber band to make your paddle.

Step 5: Twist the paddle backward, set the boat in water, and let go! The untwisting motion will spin the paddle and move the boat.
