🌿 Community Events

WeAreBrampton: About Us

WeAreBrampton is a community-rooted initiative built to amplify local voices, nurture empathy, and spark conversations that bring people closer together. What began as a research project aimed at understanding what Brampton residents need when it comes to community and well-being, soon grew into something much more. It became a real-life space where people can gather, listen, reflect, and connect.

🌳 The WeAreBrampton Walk with Nature

Featured Event

The WeAreBrampton Nature Walk is a new step in our journey, quite literally! This event is about getting outside, connecting with each other and the land we live on, and learning together. It's especially designed with families and kids in mind, with fun, STEM-inspired activities that encourage creativity, curiosity and collaboration.

At its heart, the walk is about building community. We want to spark joy, conversation, and connection in a way that's simple but meaningful.

📅 Event Details

Date: Sunday, August 10th
Time: TBD (afternoon)
Location: Churchville Park
Who: All ages welcome — perfect for kids and families!
Cost: Free!

🎒 Extra Details for the Walk

To make sure you have the best experience possible, here are a few things to keep in mind:

👟
Wear comfy shoes — we'll be walking along natural trails.
💧
Bring a filled water bottle to stay hydrated.
☀️
Apply sunscreen and consider bringing bug spray to stay comfortable.
🍎
We'll be providing light snacks to keep your energy up.
🌧️
In case of rainy weather, the walk will be rescheduled—we'll let you know ahead of time.

We want this to be a relaxed and enjoyable time for everyone, so feel free to bring anything else that makes you feel comfortable outdoors!
