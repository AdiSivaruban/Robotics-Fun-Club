üåü About Robotics Fun Club

Welcome to Our STEM Adventure!

The Robotics Fun Club is a community workshop and website for younger kids to explore basic robotics and science concepts using simple, everyday materials like batteries, paperclips, foil, cardboard, and rubber bands. Our activities help kids get excited about robotics and learning without screens.

Founded by a passionate educator with experience teaching piano and helping run summer camps, our club brings together the joy of hands-on learning with the excitement of robotics. We've discovered that the best way to learn about science and technology is through getting your hands dirty and building real things!

üéØ Our Mission

We're on a mission to make robotics and STEM education accessible to every child in our local community, regardless of their background or resources. Using simple materials you already have at home, we show kids they don't need expensive kits to explore the wonders of science and engineering.

üèÜ What Makes Us Special

Screen-Free Learning: All our projects encourage hands-on building and real-world problem solving

Everyday Materials: Use items you already have at home

Progressive Difficulty: Start easy and build up to more complex projects

Safety First: All projects include clear safety guidelines

Community Focus: Share your creations and get inspired by others through our photo upload feature

Join us on this incredible journey of discovery, where science meets fun, and every project is a new adventure waiting to unfold!
