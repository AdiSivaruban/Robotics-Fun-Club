Rubber Band Boat

Easy
⏱️ 20 min

Build a self-propelled boat using a plastic bottle and rubber band!

📦 Materials ▼
✓ 1 empty plastic water bottle
✓ 3 popsicle sticks
✓ 1 strong rubber band
✓ Tape

Step 1: Prepare the Hull

Use the plastic bottle as your boat body. Make sure it's sealed and floats. Decorate it however you want!

Step 2: Create Paddle Support

Tape two popsicle sticks parallel to each other near the back of the bottle with a small gap between them.

Step 3: Add Rubber Band

Stretch the rubber band between the two popsicle sticks. Make sure it's tight but not about to snap.

Step 4: Make the Paddle

Cut a popsicle stick in half and slide it through the rubber band. Tape it together.

Step 5: Launch!

Wind the paddle backward, place in water, and release! Watch your boat zoom across the water!

Robot Arm Grabber

Medium
⏱️ 45 min

Create a mechanical grabber that works like a real robot arm!

📦 Materials ▼
✓ 1 wooden ruler
✓ Cardboard strips
✓ 4 paper fasteners
✓ Tape & scissors
✓ Rubber band & string

Step 1: Prepare Cardboard

Cut three long strips and two short strips. Punch holes near each end for connections.

Step 2: Build Scissor Mechanism

Cross two long strips into an "X" and connect with a paper fastener at the center.

Step 3: Attach to Base

Connect the third strip to the ruler and attach short strips for linkages.

Step 4: Add Spring System

Hook rubber band between pivot points for automatic closing action.

Step 5: Add Control String

Tie string to rubber band. Pull to open, release to close!

Bottle Rocket

Advanced
⏱️ 30 min

Launch a rocket using air pressure! Adult supervision required.

⚠️ Safety First

  • Always do this outdoors
  • Adult supervision required
  • Never point at people or property
📦 Materials ▼
✓ 1 empty plastic bottle with lid
✓ 3 pencils
✓ Hairspray or spray deodorant
✓ BBQ lighter
✓ Tape

Step 1: Prepare Rocket Body

Puncture a small hole in the bottle cap. Decorate your rocket however you like!

Step 2: Build Launch Base

Attach three pencils around the bottle to form a stable tripod base.

Step 3: Add Fuel

Spray a short burst of hairspray inside and seal with cap. Shake for 5-10 seconds.

Step 4: Launch Sequence

Hold lighter near (not touching) the hole. The vapor will ignite and launch!

Balloon Car

Easy
⏱️ 25 min

Build a car powered by escaping air from a balloon!

📦 Materials ▼
✓ Plastic bottle
✓ 4 bottle caps
✓ Skewers
✓ Straw & balloon
✓ Tape

Step 1: Make Wheels

Push skewers through bottle caps to create two axles with wheels.

Step 2: Attach Axles

Poke holes in bottle and secure axles so wheels spin freely.

Step 3: Prepare Balloon System

Insert straw into balloon opening and tape securely.

Step 4: Mount and Race!

Tape balloon to car with straw pointing backward. Inflate, release, and race!
