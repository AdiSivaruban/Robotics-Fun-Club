Rubber Band Boat

Easy
‚è±Ô∏è 20 min

Build a self-propelled boat using a plastic bottle and rubber band!

Materials: Plastic bottle, popsicle sticks, rubber band, tape
View Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Hull

Use the plastic bottle as your boat body. Make sure it's sealed and floats.

Step 2: Create Paddle Support

Tape two popsicle sticks parallel to each other near the back of the bottle with a small gap between them.

Step 3: Add Rubber Band

Stretch the rubber band between the two popsicle sticks.

Step 4: Make the Paddle

Cut a popsicle stick in half and slide it through the rubber band. Tape it together.

Step 5: Launch!

Wind the paddle backward, place in water, and release!

Robot Arm Grabber

Medium
‚è±Ô∏è 45 min

Create a mechanical grabber that works like a real robot arm!

Materials: Wooden ruler, cardboard, paper fasteners, rubber band, string
View Instructions

Step 1: Prepare Cardboard

Cut three long strips and two short strips. Punch holes near each end.

Step 2: Build Scissor Mechanism

Cross two long strips into an "X" and connect with a paper fastener at the center.

Step 3: Attach to Base

Connect the third strip to the ruler and attach the short strips to create linkages.

Step 4: Add Spring Mechanism

Hook rubber band between pivot points to create automatic closing action.

Step 5: Add Control String

Tie string to rubber band. Pull to open, release to close!

Bottle Rocket

Advanced
‚è±Ô∏è 30 min

Launch a rocket using air pressure! Adult supervision required.

Materials: Plastic bottle, pencils, hairspray, BBQ lighter, tape

‚ö†Ô∏è Safety First

  • Always do this outdoors
  • Adult supervision required
  • Never point at people or property
  • Keep flammable materials away
View Instructions

Step 1: Prepare Rocket

Puncture a small hole in the bottle cap. Decorate your rocket body.

Step 2: Build Launch Base

Attach three pencils around the bottle to form a tripod base.

Step 3: Add Fuel

Spray a short burst of hairspray inside and seal with cap.

Step 4: Launch

Hold lighter near (not touching) the hole. The vapor will ignite and launch the rocket!

Balloon Car

Easy
‚è±Ô∏è 25 min

Build a car powered by escaping air from a balloon!

Materials: Plastic bottle, bottle caps, skewers, straw, balloon, tape
View Instructions

Step 1: Make Wheels

Push skewers through bottle caps to create two axles with wheels.

Step 2: Attach Axles

Poke holes in bottle and secure axles so wheels spin freely.

Step 3: Prepare Balloon

Insert straw into balloon opening and tape securely.

Step 4: Mount Balloon

Tape balloon assembly to car with straw pointing backward.

Step 5: Race!

Inflate balloon, pinch straw, place car down, and release!
